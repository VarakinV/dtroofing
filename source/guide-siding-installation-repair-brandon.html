Siding Installation & Repair in Brandon

Choosing, installing and repairing siding in the Westman region — including when a repair beats a full replacement.

D.T Roofing · Brandon, MB

Siding is what protects your walls from prairie weather and what your house looks like from the street. Here’s what to know about installing, repairing and choosing it.

Why siding matters

Good siding keeps water and wind off the structure and finishes the look of the home. Tired, cracked or storm-damaged siding lets moisture in and drags down curb appeal — and it rarely gets better on its own.

Your siding options

  • Vinyl — affordable, low-maintenance and available in a wide range of colours
  • Engineered wood / lap siding — a richer, more substantial look
  • Accents — shake panels and stone to give the exterior character

We install and repair siding as part of finishing the whole exterior. See siding & eavestrough.

Hail-damaged siding and PVC windows

Cracked vinyl siding and PVC windows are common after a storm — but a full replacement isn’t always necessary. Our sister company, Fibrenew Brandon, repairs vinyl, leather and plastic, which can save you the cost of replacing whole sections. When repair makes more sense than replacement, we’ll tell you.

Don’t forget the eavestrough

Siding works hand in hand with eavestrough, soffit and fascia to move water away from the home. Because we handle the roof and the whole exterior, it all gets detailed to work together — see our eavestrough guide.

The bottom line: whether it’s a full re-side, storm repair, or just freshening the look, we’ll quote it free and point you to the option that actually fits.
