Why metal

The Last Roof You'll Buy

Out here the real test of a roof is wind, and metal — installed correctly — is the best answer to it. It's also why so many farm buildings still wear their original metal decades on.

We help you choose the right system for your building and budget, then install it to last. Whether it's a home, an acreage, or a commercial building, you get a roof that handles Manitoba weather instead of fighting it.

  • Holds up in prairie wind when it's detailed right at the eaves
  • Sheds snow on its own and shrugs off hail
  • Lasts decades — the long way out of the asphalt re-do cycle
  • A range of colours and profiles to suit the house

Done right

The Right Metal for the Building

Not all metal is maintenance-free. The systems that are belong on your house; the ones that need ongoing upkeep are better suited to outbuildings — and we'll tell you honestly which is which.

Standing seam & snap-lock

Concealed-fastener panels — the genuine maintenance-free, lifetime roof for homes and heated buildings. No exposed screws to back out over time.

Metal shingles

Want the look of slate or shake with the life of metal? Metal shingles give you a premium finish on a home — including Metstar Slate Plus (below).

Outbuildings

Exposed-fastener panels (Ag-style) belong on barns, shops and garages — not heated homes, where the screws and gaskets need re-doing over time. We'll tell you straight which one your building needs.

What sets us apart

We Roll-Form Our Own Standing Seam

Most "metal roofers" only install panels they buy in from a supplier. We roll-form our standing-seam panels ourselves, on site.

That means panels cut to your roof's exact lengths — fewer seams, a cleaner fit, and real control over the wind detailing that makes a metal roof actually last on a heated home. It's a genuine capability, not a line on a website.

  • Panels made to your exact roof lengths
  • Control over the fit, the finish and the wind detailing
  • Fully insured, workmanship guaranteed
Metstar Slate Plus — the metal shingle we install

A 26-gauge ZincAlum metal shingle with the look of slate and a 50-year lifespan that installs right over your existing shingles. The premium metal-shingle option for a home.
