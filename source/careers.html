Join the crew

Build a Career, Not Just a Job

We're a third-generation, owner-operated outfit, and we're always glad to hear from people who take pride in their work. We don't always have a spot open the day you reach out — but we keep good applications on hand, and we'll teach the trade to anyone willing to learn it.

Wage-based training

A paid program that rewards drive and skill over seniority — high achievers advance quickly.

Benefits you'll actually use

A flex benefits plan you split your way — a Health Spending Account for medical and dental, plus a Wellness Spending Account for the gym, sports gear, golf, even your phone, or RRSP top-ups. Healthy or not, none of it goes to waste.

No degree, no debt

No college required. Learn a real trade on the job, get paid while you do it, and grow across roofing, metal, siding and more. Why take on debt when you can learn a skill?
