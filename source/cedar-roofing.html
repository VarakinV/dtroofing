A Roof With Real Character

Nothing else looks like cedar. It's the classic choice for character homes, cottages and lake places — a roof with warmth and texture you can't fake.

Cedar rewards a careful install. Done right — with proper breathing room under the wood and clean detailing — it's a beautiful, long-serving roof. We install cedar shakes and shingles, and repair and replace existing cedar roofs too.
