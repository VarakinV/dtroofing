Clear Lake & area

Your Clear Lake Roofers

Clear Lake and the Riding Mountain area are cottage and resort country north of Brandon. Cabins, year-round homes and steep cottage roofs all have to handle heavy snow — exactly where a properly installed metal or shingle roof earns its keep.

Metal is our lead product — built for prairie wind and snow — but we do it all: shingle re-roofs, repairs, siding and eavestrough, plus honest help through a storm or hail claim. Free quotes, and a straight answer either way.

  • Siding, eavestrough & soffit
