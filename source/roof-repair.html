Straight answers

When to Repair — and When Not To

A repair is the right call more often than people think. But not always — and we'll tell you which one you're looking at.

If the shingles around the damage have lost their seal, a patch won't hold past the next big wind, and you'd just be paying twice. When that's the case, we'll say so. When a repair will genuinely do the job, we'll do that instead of selling you a roof you don't need.

  • Honest verdict — repair, re-roof, or leave it alone
  • No upsell and no scare tactics
  • Storm damage? We can document it for an insurance claim
