A proper look

See What’s Actually Up There

Hail damage often isn’t visible from the ground, and small problems become expensive ones when they’re left. A proper inspection tells you what’s real.

We’re HAAG Certified Inspectors, so storm and hail damage gets assessed and documented the way it should be — especially on a roof over 15 years old after a confirmed storm.

  • Storm, hail and wind damage assessment
  • General condition and age check
  • HAAG certified — documented properly
  • Ties straight into your insurance claim if there’s damage

What we check

What an Inspection Covers

Storm & hail

After a confirmed storm, we check for hail bruising, wind-lifted shingles and the damage you can’t see from the yard.

Condition & age

Seals, flashings, valleys and ventilation — a straight read on how much life the roof has left.

Documentation

Findings recorded properly — the basis for a fair insurance scope if there’s a claim to make.
